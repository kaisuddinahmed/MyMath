**Curriculum Standard:** NCTB Bangladesh (Primary Level, Classes 1-5)

---

## Class 1
### 1. Number Sense & Numeration
* **Counting:** Objects and visual identification (1-100).
* **Place Value:** Introduction to Tens and Ones.
* **Comparisons:** Visual sizes (Big/Small) and numeric value ($<$, $>$, $=$).

### 2. Core Arithmetic
* **Basic Addition:** Sums up to 100 (without complex carrying).
* **Basic Subtraction:** Differences within 100 (without complex borrowing).

### 3. Geometry & Patterns
* **2D Shapes:** Identification of Triangles, Rectangles, and Circles.
* **Patterns:** Simple visual and numeric sequences.

### 4. Applied Mathematics
* **Currency:** Visual recognition of Bangladeshi notes and coins.

---

## Class 2
### 1. Number Sense & Numeration
* **Expanded Counting:** Numbers up to 1,000.
* **Number Properties:** Even and Odd numbers (Jor/Bijor).
* **Ordering:** Ordinal numbers (1st to 10th).

### 2. Core Arithmetic
* **Advanced Addition:** 2-3 digit sums with "Carrying".
* **Advanced Subtraction:** 2-3 digit differences with "Borrowing".
* **Basic Multiplication:** Concept of repeated addition, Times Tables (1-10).
* **Basic Division:** Equal distribution (strictly no remainders).

### 3. Fractions (Visual)
* **Unit Fractions:** Visual recognition of One-Half ($1/2$) and One-Quarter ($1/4$).

### 4. Measurement
* **Physical Units:** Length (m/cm), Weight (kg/gm).
* **Time:** Reading analog clocks (Hours/Minutes), Days of the week, Months.

### 5. Geometry
* **Lines & Shapes:** Straight vs. Curved lines, counting sides and corners of 2D shapes.

---

## Class 3
### 1. Number Sense & Numeration
* **Large Numbers:** Place value up to 100,000 (Lakh/Ayut).
* **Advanced Ordinals:** 11th to 20th.

### 2. Core Arithmetic
* **Long Multiplication:** 2-to-3 digit numbers by 1-to-2 digit numbers.
* **Division with Remainders:** Finding explicit Quotients and Remainders.

### 3. Algebraic Thinking
* **Inverse Operations:** Solving for missing numbers (e.g., if $\square + 5 = 12$, then $12 - 5 = \square$).

### 4. Applied Mathematics
* **Multi-step Word Problems:** Using the Unitary Method (e.g., total cost, then calculating change).
* **Currency Math:** Operations converting between Taka and Poisa ($100p = 1Tk$).

### 5. Geometry
* **Angles & Elements:** Identifying Points, Angles, and specific attributes of Quadrilaterals.

### 6. Data Handling
* **Tally Marks:** Organizing raw data into tally tables.

---

## Class 4
### 1. Number Sense & Numeration
* **Mega Numbers:** Crores (National) and Millions (International system).

### 2. Number Theory
* **Multiples & Factors:** Generating lists.
* **LCM & HCF:** Lowest Common Multiple and Highest Common Factor.
* **Divisibility:** Rules for basic factors (2, 3, 5, etc.).

### 3. Algebraic Thinking
* **Mathematical Sentences:** Evaluating open sentences and inequalities.
* **Basic BODMAS:** Order of operations introducing Parentheses `()`.

### 4. Fractions & Decimals
* **Common Fractions:** Equivalent fractions, Addition/Subtraction (Same Denominator).
* **Decimal Fractions:** Tenths ($0.1$) and Hundredths ($0.01$), Decimal Add/Sub.

### 5. Measurement & Geometry
* **Advanced Time:** 12-hour vs 24-hour clock conversions.
* **Angle Classification:** Acute, Right, Obtuse, and Straight angles.
* **Lines:** Parallel and Perpendicular lines.

### 6. Data Handling
* **Graphing:** Interpreting and reading Bar Graphs.

---

## Class 5
### 1. Advanced Arithmetic
* **Complex Operations:** 5-digit by 3-digit multiplication and division.

### 2. Algebraic Thinking
* **Full BODMAS:** Order of operations with nested brackets: `()`, `{}`, and `[]`.

### 3. Number Theory
* **Prime Factorization:** Advanced LCM/HCF using prime trees and word problems.

### 4. Fractions & Decimals
* **Advanced Common Fractions:** Operations with Mixed and Improper fractions (Different Denominators).
* **Advanced Decimals:** Multiplying and Dividing decimals by decimals.

### 5. Applied & Commercial Mathematics
* **Percentage:** Converting to/from fractions, calculating `%` of a number.
* **Commercial Math:** Profit, Loss, and Simple Interest.
* **Averages:** Calculating the mean of datasets.

### 6. Measurement & Geometry
* **Area:** Calculating Area for Rectangles, Squares, and Triangles.
* **Quadrilaterals:** Distinguishing Rhombus, Parallelogram, Trapezium.
* **Circles:** Identifying Radius, Diameter, and Chord.

### 7. Data Handling
* **Histograms:** Constructing frequency tables with class intervals and reading histograms.
