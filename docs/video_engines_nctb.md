# MyMath Video Engine Mapping

This document maps the NCTB Class 1-5 curriculum to the 5 highly-reusable Remotion engines to ensure scalable and engaging educational content.

## The 5 Visual Engines

1.  **The Object Array (Class 1-2 Focus)**
    *   *Visuals:* Bouncy, discrete SVG objects (apples, blocks).
    *   *Capabilities:* Counting, appearing, basic grouping (addition), disappearing/separating (subtraction), patterns.
2.  **The Vertical Grid (Class 2-4 Focus)**
    *   *Visuals:* A strict CSS grid for single numbers.
    *   *Capabilities:* Multi-digit addition/subtraction, carrying/borrowing, vertical multiplication/division.
3.  **The Relationship Engine (Class 3-5 Focus)**
    *   *Visuals:* Horizontal comparisons, number lines, highlighted lists.
    *   *Capabilities:* LCM/HCF, Odd/Even rules, ordering numbers.
4.  **The Fraction / Shape Drawer (Class 3-5 Focus)**
    *   *Visuals:* Smoothly filling SVGs (circles, rectangles, lines).
    *   *Capabilities:* Fractions, decimals (using grids), basic geometry drawing.
5.  **The Abstract Manipulator (Class 4-5 Focus)**
    *   *Visuals:* Horizontal equations that slide, shrink, and replace parts of themselves.
    *   *Capabilities:* BODMAS, Mathematical Sentences, advanced order of operations.

## Class-by-Class Mapping

### Class 1 (Concrete Foundations)
* **Goal:** Visual understanding of quantities.
* **Engine Used:** Primarily **The Object Array**.
* **Mapping:**
  * Chapter 1 (Comparison), 2 (Counting), 3/6/9/14 (Numbers 1-100): *Object Array* popping in with sounds.
  * Chapter 4/7/15 (Addition): *Object Array* merging groups.
  * Chapter 5/8/16 (Subtraction): *Object Array* items shrinking/fading away.
  * Chapter 12 (Geometry): *Shape Drawer* showing 2D shapes.
  * Chapter 13 (Pattern): *Object Array* with sequential SVGs (e.g., Apple, Banana, Apple, Banana).

### Class 2 (Transition to Algorithms)
* **Goal:** Moving from objects to vertical columns.
* **Engines Used:** Mix of **Object Array** and **Vertical Grid**.
* **Mapping:**
  * Chapter 1 (Numbers/Place Value): *Object Array* (10-blocks and 1-blocks).
  * Chapter 2 (Addition & Subtraction): *Vertical Grid* to introduce column math.
  * Chapter 3 (Multiplication): *Object Array* arranging into grids/arrays.
  * Chapter 4 (Shapes/Patterns): *Shape Drawer*.
  * Chapter 5-7 (Measurement, Currency, Data): Simple *Object Arrays* using specific SVGs (coins, rulers).

### Class 3 (Abstract Representation)
* **Goal:** Mastery of multi-digit vertical math and intro to fractions.
* **Engines Used:** **Vertical Grid** and **Fraction Drawer**.
* **Mapping:**
  * Chapter 1 (Numbers): *Vertical Grid* sliding into Place Value columns.
  * Chapter 2-6 (Core Operations): Purely *Vertical Grid* with carrying/borrowing animations.
  * Chapter 9 (Fractions): **Fraction Drawer** (circles filling up).
  * Chapter 10-13 (Currency, Measurement, Geometry, Data): *Shape Drawer* and *Object Array*.

### Class 4 (Rules & Relationships)
* **Goal:** Mathematical structure and logic.
* **Engines Used:** **Abstract Manipulator** and **Relationship Engine**.
* **Mapping:**
  * Chapter 1-4 (Core Operations): *Vertical Grid*.
  * Chapter 5 (Mathematical Sentence): **Abstract Manipulator** (BODMAS introductions, closing gaps).
  * Chapter 6 (Multiples & Factors): **Relationship Engine** (highlighting common numbers in two lists).
  * Chapter 7-8 (Fractions & Decimals): *Fraction Drawer* combined with *Vertical Grid*.
  * Chapter 9-11 (Measurement, Geometry, Data): *Shape Drawer*.

### Class 5 (Advanced Application)
* **Goal:** Complex multi-step problem solving.
* **Engines Used:** **Abstract Manipulator**, **Fraction Drawer**, and **Vertical Grid**.
* **Mapping:**
  * Chapter 1 (Mul/Div): *Vertical Grid*.
  * Chapter 2 (Mathematical Sentences): **Abstract Manipulator** (Deeply nested BODMAS).
  * Chapter 3 (Multiples/Factors): *Relationship Engine*.
  * Chapter 4-5 (Fractions): *Fraction Drawer* (showing logic, not just pies).
  * Chapter 6-7 (Percentage, Average): *Fraction Drawer* (100-grids) and *Object Array* (bar graphs leveling out).
  * Chapter 8-10 (Measurement, Geometry, Data): *Shape Drawer* (programmatic angles and lines).
